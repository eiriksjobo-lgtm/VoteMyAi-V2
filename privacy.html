Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you log in with Google, we collect your name and email address.

Content: Tracks you submit, votes you cast, and comments you post.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use VoteMyAI, including pages visited and features used.

Local Storage: We store vote data in your browser's local storage to prevent duplicate voting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Provide and improve our services
• Display your submitted tracks and comments
• Prevent voting fraud and spam
• Communicate with you about your account
• Analyze platform usage and trends

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

• Service Providers: Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), Google (authentication)
• Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access your personal data
• Delete your account and submitted content
• Opt out of communications
• Request data corrections

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage to track votes and maintain your login session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

VoteMyAI is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact us through our Contact page.
