Frequently Asked Questions

What is VoteMyAI?
VoteMyAI is a platform where you can submit your AI-generated music tracks and let the community vote on them. The best tracks rise to the top!
How do I submit a track?
Click "Submit Track" in the navigation, log in with your Google account, and fill out the submission form with your YouTube link, track title, AI tool used, and genre.
Do I need to log in to vote?
No! You can vote without logging in. However, logging in allows you to submit tracks and comment on them.
Can I vote multiple times on the same track?
No, you can only vote once per track. We track votes locally in your browser to prevent spam.
Which AI music tools are supported?
We support tracks created with Suno, Udio, ElevenLabs, and other AI music generation tools. Just select your tool when submitting.
Can I delete my submitted tracks?
Yes! Go to your profile page and click the delete button next to any of your tracks.
How does the leaderboard work?
The leaderboard shows the top 10 tracks with the most votes. It updates in real-time as people vote.
